# Dirtwork Insurance Domain Research and Development

## Domain Overview
- **Domain**: dirtworkinsurance.com
- **Focus**: Insurance for excavation and dirt work contractors
- **Status**: Domain owned but not yet developed
- **Priority**: High - Identified as a growth opportunity in the construction sector

## Market Potential
Dirtwork insurance represents a specialized niche within contractor insurance with good growth potential. Excavation and dirt work contractors require specialized coverage for their unique risks related to earth moving, grading, trenching, and site preparation.

## Current Status
Currently the domain is owned but has minimal or no content. The domain has been prioritized for development based on the strength of the construction industry and limited specialized competition in this niche.

## Development Goals
This folder contains research, planning documents, and content development for the dirtworkinsurance.com website.
